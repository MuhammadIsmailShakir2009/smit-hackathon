Job Title: Data Analyst

We are looking for a Data Analyst with 3+ years of experience to join our growing analytics team.

Required Skills:
- Python and SQL for data analysis
- Machine learning fundamentals
- Data visualization using Power BI or Tableau
- Strong communication and problem solving skills
- Experience with Excel and data cleaning

Education: Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, or related field. Master's degree is a plus.

Responsibilities:
- Analyze large datasets to generate business insights
- Build dashboards and reports for stakeholders
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams on data-driven projects
- Present findings to management in a clear, actionable format

We value candidates who are adaptable, detail-oriented, and passionate about turning data into decisions.
